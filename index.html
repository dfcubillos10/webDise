Overwatch

On early 2017 we created the team with a couple of friends. After an autocorrect keyboard turned Larumonmon (one of our members) to Lululemons, the name was decided.

Legend said that a team known as Evolve was unbeatable here in Colombia, and after seing that they were so talented, yet had some awful manners, we decided to take them down

We trained for months, made and watched our vods, and global competitive Overwatch streams, tips and strategies.

We finally accomplished what was thought imposible: We beat Evolve and became Colombia's best Overwatch team at the 2017 SOFA's National Chamionship
